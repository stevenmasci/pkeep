Features Pricing

Investment properties made simple

Keep track of your investment properties, transactions and documents with ease.

Save yourself the headache when tax time comes

Collating expenses and receipts for the past financial year can be a pain, with pTrack you can easily stay on top of items as they come in.

Soft copies

Send or forward transactions including attachments to a generated personal email address to automatically add them to your folio.



Hard copies

Use the pTrack mobile app and your phone's camera to scan documents and instantly add them to a transaction.

Collaborate with others when managing a joint property

Give others access to properties in your folio and maintain a unified transaction list, shared responsibility and management. Avoid doubling up on payments, documents and receipts.

See where you financially stand for the year and historically

Know when you are cash flow positive, neutral, or negative for individual properties and your entire portfolio. See how you financially stand in comparison to previous financial years.

Financial summaries

Download end of year financial summaries containing an itemised list of all your transactions and monthly totals. Contains everything your tax agent needs to know.

Export your folio

You're not locked into using pTrack for life, export your complete folio at any time. All transactions, attachments and financial summaries will be neatly bundled together.

Keeping your information safe

Every transaction, document and attachment you add to pTrack is securely encrypted and stored using the best security practices.

All data is transferred over HTTPS utilising Transport Layer Security, the most widely used security protocol, meaning the connection between pTrack and you is private and secure.

All data is stored in compliance-certified data centers adhering to internationally recognised security standards including ISO 27001.

For added peace of mind you can fully export or delete your complete folio and data at any time.
